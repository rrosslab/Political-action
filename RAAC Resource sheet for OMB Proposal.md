[Government site for posting comment](https://www.regulations.gov/document/OMB-2026-0034-0001?utm_campaign=ATS%20Stat%20Newsletter&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-8GH_Ncsg5l-EmC0n3fljDy93pBYW9mS_vozYYs3hfeT7hM4HuPrPQEdRYUConPbzvQOeycMUVK2kyEQMzdHZa3nd48GA&_hsmi=2&utm_content=2&utm_source=hs_email).

Must include:

- 5000 character limit per comment, but you can attach a written statement, or you can write multiple comments, as long as they are unique and different points.
- Explain your concerns by section number - must include section number!
- State what you want the OMB to do (don't pass the provision/don't finalize the rule)
- Personalized comments are most effective
- You can post anonymously, but including a contact email will help you follow the response to your commentary, which must legally be addressed by OMB.

[Proposal to Cap the Number of Simultaneous Research Project Grants per Principal Investigator](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/guide/notice-files/NOT-OD-26-086.html)

\-The NIH is proposing capping the number of simultaneous research projects for each PI at 2-4 grants

\-This could have large implications on PI's ability to reach independence and support their salaries with NIH dollars

\-Could potentially add extra stress on institutions to cover that gap
