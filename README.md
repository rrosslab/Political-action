# Political-action
This is public available resource for anyone interested in public engagement as it relates to science and politics. Resource pages from various advocacy groups and how-to-docs will be shared here, made by RR through the Research and Academic Advocacy Committee. The RAAC resource sheet has a number of links to explainer articles and videos about what is in the proposal and how to prep a comment. The other documents come from Research America, and include similar resources produced by their group in conjunction with a law firm that have come out more recently.
